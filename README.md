#### Course:

#### Team Members:
Azam, Brandon, David, Trey

# Description:
    GameHub.io is a centralized adless gaming platform, which allows users to play their
favorite games, track their scores, and be ranked against their friends and peers. Many current websites don’t include nostalgic games from our childhood like Hangman and Snake or have features for users to easily track their progress and compare their performance against their friends. GameHub.io solves all of that by creating a centralized website with everyone’s favorite nostalgic games, and friendly competition amongst friends, with an ad-free experience.
